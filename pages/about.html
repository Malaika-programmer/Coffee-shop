Our Story

From a single bean to your daily cup of joy.

Brewing Since 2015

Coffee Break wasn't just built on business plans; it was built on a passion for connection. What started as a humble corner shop has grown into a community hub where creativity meets caffeine.

We believe that a great cup of coffee can turn a bad day around. That's why we source only the top 1% of Arabica beans and roast them locally to ensure peak freshness.

"We don't just serve coffee; we serve moments of pause in a chaotic world."
- Malaika Azam, Founder

Our Core Values
